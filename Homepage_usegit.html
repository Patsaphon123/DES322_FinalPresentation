RAMEN LOVER ラメン



The unique taste of this Tokyo import was created by Ramen Lover, who introduced ramen under the concept of “farm to bowl”. Ingredients are sourced from local farmers while specialty items are flown in from Japan. What’s in each bowl? Fresh, handmade noodles, sous vide or fire-roasted meats and a hot milky broth.Get a taste of Ramen Lover, a secret ramen hub . Homemade ramen noodles are produced everyday and served with rich soups made with tamari (Japanese soy sauce) and miso.More an udon place than a ramen restaurant.
